Fifa 15


Fifa 15 is an association football simulation video game developed by EA Canadaand published by Electronic Arts.
It was released on 23 September 2014 in North America,25 September in Europe and 26 September
in the United Kingdom and Ireland for the PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4,
PlayStation Vita, Nintendo 3DS, Wii, Xbox One, Xbox 360,
Microsoft Windows, iOS,Android and Windows Phone.
On PC for the first time,FIFA 15 runs on EA's
Ignite engine with the same features as the
PlayStation 4 and Xbox One editions.
