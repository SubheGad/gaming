The game is set during World War II
and the campaign mode is experienced
through the perspectives of four soldiers:
one in the Red Army, one in the United States Army,
and two in the British Army.
It contains four individual campaigns,
split into three stories, with a total of 27 missions.
Many features were added and changed from the
original Call of Duty, notably regenerating health
and an icon that indicates a nearby grenade
about to explode.
Call Of Duty 2
Call of Duty 2 features several game types:
Deathmatch, Team Deathmatch, Search & Destroy,
Capture the Flag and Headquarters.
The maps include Normandy, Africa, and Russia.
Each team can choose a variety of weapons,
depending on the map.
Players can choose between American soldiers,
oviet soldiers, and British soldiers for the Allied forces,
while the Axis forces must play as German soldiers.
