Described as "soccer, but with rocket-powered cars", Rocket League has one to four players assigned to each
of the two teams, using rocket-powered vehicles to hit a ball into their opponent's goal and score points over
the course of a match. The game includes single-player and multiplayer modes which can be played both locally
and online, including cross-platform play between the PlayStation 4 and Microsoft Windows versions,
as well as cross-play between Xbox One, Switch, and Windows versions.
