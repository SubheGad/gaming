Fortnite
Currently, Fortnite provides two distinct modes:
A Player-Versus-Environment "Save the World",
And a Player-Versus-Player "Battle Royale".
The latter mode was added after Fortnite's initial early access launch,
And later offered as a separate free-to-play mode on September 26, 2017,
That does not require the base game to play.
